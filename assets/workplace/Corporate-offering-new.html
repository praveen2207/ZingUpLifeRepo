ABOUT ZINGUPLIFE

ZingUpLife is an online discovery platform, and marketplace for guided wellness services

  • Bundled with real-time engagement with a wide ranging panel of SMEs
  • Service Categories include Spa’s, Ayurveda, Yoga, Fitness & Naturopathy
  • Comprehensive services listing for user discovery, booking functionality for curated services
  • Content repository, backed by collated knowledge and wisdom of global wellness evangelists
  • Crowd sourced ratings and reviews

Workplace Wellness Programs launched at Bangalore, in Feb 2016

  • Holistic wellness solutions for the workplace
  • Emphasis on stress management, complete wellbeing, and leading a healthier life ‘in and outside’ of office
  • Bespoke sessions and programs suited for employers of all shapes and sizes
Subsequent geographies, in FY 2016 – 17 include Delhi NCR, Mumbai/ Pune/ Goa, Chaudhuriennai/ Hyderabad, followed by South East Asia
